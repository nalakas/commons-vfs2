The Apache Commons VFS Sandbox module is not officially released by the Apache Commons project.
If you chose to compile it manually with `mvn -Pinclude-sandbox verify` it will require the following
additional dependencies with licenses not approved for redistribution:

 +- jcifs:jcifs:jar:0.8.3:compile
 +- javax.mail:mail:jar:1.4.7:compile
    \- javax.activation:activation:jar:1.1:compile
